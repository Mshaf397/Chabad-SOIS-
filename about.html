About Us

Welcome to Chabad SOIS! We’re a warm, welcoming community dedicated to spreading Jewish knowledge and joy. Whether you’re new or experienced, young or old, there’s a place for you here. We aim to foster a sense of belonging and connection for everyone in the local area.

Our mission is simple: to inspire and support the local Jewish community through meaningful events, learning opportunities, and caring outreach, serving the spiritual and material needs of all Jews.

Come join us for lively holiday celebrations, engaging classes, Shabbat dinners, and more. We believe in unconditional love and acceptance, making Jewish life vibrant and accessible to all. We look forward to meeting you!
