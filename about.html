About Us

Welcome to Chabad SOIS! We’re a warm, welcoming community dedicated to spreading Jewish knowledge and joy. Whether you’re new or experienced, young or old, there’s a place for you here.

Our mission is to inspire and support the local Jewish community through meaningful events, learning opportunities, and caring outreach.

Come join us for holiday celebrations, classes, and more — we look forward to meeting you!
