The Power of Shabbat

Shabbat, the weekly day of rest, is a powerful opportunity to step away from the hustle and bustle of daily life and connect with family, friends, and spirituality. It is a time to reflect, recharge, and appreciate the blessings we often overlook during the week.

Learn more at The 39 Melachot.

Join us every Friday night at Chabad SOIS for a warm and welcoming Shabbat dinner. Experience the beauty of tradition, prayer, and good food with community members of all backgrounds.
